Applying for a Virtual Data Center (VDC)

Introduction

A virtual data center (VDC) is the unit using virtual resources in ServiceCenter. Applying for a VDC indicates the process that the VDC administrator applies for a VDC from the administrator as a service to manage resources (such as VMs) in the VDC.

Prerequisites

VDC service that meet the application requirements already exist in the service list. If not, the administrator must create related services first.
