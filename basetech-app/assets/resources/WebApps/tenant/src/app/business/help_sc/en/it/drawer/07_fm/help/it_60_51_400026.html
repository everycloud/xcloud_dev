Authorization

If you want to allow an administrator to manage resources in your virtual private cloud (VPC), use this function to grant authorization to the administrator. After the authorization takes effect, the administrator is allowed to perform operations in VMs in your VPC, including modifying VMs, exporting templates from VMs, attaching USB devices to VMs, detaching USB devices from VMs, and creating or importing VMs to the VPC.

Grant Authorization

Grant the administrator to manage the resources in your VPC.

Terminate Authorization

Terminate the authorization for a VPC.
