Catalog Management

A service catalog is used to store services that have the same properties. Classifying services using a service catalog makes management and maintenance more convenient for the administrator.

Creating a Catalog

A catalog is used to manage services by classification. A catalog can store one or multiple services.

Click Create Catalog on the Catalog Management page.

Modifying a Catalog

You can modify the name and description of a catalog.

Click Modify in Operation from the row where the catalog locates on the Catalog Management page.

Deleting a Catalog

You can delete a catalog that is no longer used. You can delete this catalog only when no service is contained in it.

Click Delete in Operation from the row where the catalog locates on the Catalog Management page.
