Equipment Rooms

The Equipment Rooms page displays information about equipment rooms housing all devices managed by FusionManager.

You can click an equipment room name to check the detailed information about the room, including the cabinets and physical devices in the room.

Adding an Equipment Room to FusionManager

You can select an existing equipment room or enter the name of your desired equipment room and add it to the system.

After an equipment room is added to FusionManager, the Equipment Rooms list displays the equipment room information.

Updating Equipment Room Information

If the attributes of an equipment room, such as its name, location, or description, have been changed after the equipment room is added to FusionManager, synchronize the new information to FusionManager in a timely manner.
