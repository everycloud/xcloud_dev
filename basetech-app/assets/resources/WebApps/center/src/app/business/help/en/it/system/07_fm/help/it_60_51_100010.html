Cabinets

The Cabinets page displays information about all cabinets managed by FusionManager.

You can click a cabinet name to check the detailed information about the cabinet, such as the physical devices residing in the cabinet.

Adding a Cabinet to FusionManager

You can select an existing cabinet or enter the name of your desired cabinet and add it to the system.

After a cabinet is added to FusionManager, the Equipment Rooms list displays the cabinet information.

Updating Cabinet Information

If the attributes of a cabinet, such as its name, location, or description, have been changed after the cabinet is added to FusionManager, synchronize the new information to FusionManager in a timely manner.

Choose Resource > Devices > Physical Location > Cabinets, locate the row that contains the target cabinet, and click Modify Cabinet Info.
