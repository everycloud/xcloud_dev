Alarm Notification by Email

In addition to the FusionManager Web Client, you can obtain alarm information from emails. After alarm notification by email is enabled, the alarm information can be sent to the listed mailboxes.

Choose Monitoring > Alarm Settings > Alarm Email Notification.
