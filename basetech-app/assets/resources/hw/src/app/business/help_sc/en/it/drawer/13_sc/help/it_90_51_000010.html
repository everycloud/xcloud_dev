All Service Instances

All Service Instances displays approved services whose status is Normal in the virtual data center (VDC) where users locate. Users can extend or delete a service.

Extending a Service

A service is usually valid only within a specified period. You can extend the service.

A service cannot be extended if the validity period of this service is Permanently Effective.

Services that must be approved by the system administrator or the VDC administrator are extended only after the extension application is approved by the administrator.

Click Extend in Operation from the row where the service instance locates.

Deleting a Service

You can delete a service when it is no longer required.

Click Delete in Operation from the row where the service instance locates.
